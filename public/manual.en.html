My Life OS is a personal life manager that integrates time, money, health, and private records into a single app. Follow this guide to get started right away.

01

Getting Started

My Life OS works directly in your web browser — no app installation required. Once you sign up, all features are immediately available.

Sign Up

1

Visit the site

Go to mylifeos-virid.vercel.app.

2

Choose Sign Up

Tap "Don't have an account? Sign up" at the bottom of the login screen.

3

Enter your details

Enter your email address and password. You can also sign up quickly with your Google account.

4

You're in!

After registration, you'll be taken to the dashboard. Start tracking your life now.

💡

Family members and friends can each sign up with their own independent account. Each person's data is completely separate and invisible to others.

02

Use It Like a Smartphone App

My Life OS can be added to your home screen without installing anything from an app store, giving you a native app experience.

iPhone (iOS Safari)

1

Open mylifeos-virid.vercel.app in Safari

2

Tap the Share button (□↑) at the bottom

3

Select "Add to Home Screen" and confirm

Android (Chrome)

1

Open the site in Chrome

2

Tap the ⋮ menu in the top right

3

Select "Add to Home Screen"

✅

Tapping the icon from your home screen launches the app in full screen without an address bar, providing the same experience as a store-installed app.

💰

Money Summary

Displays this month's total spending and budget utilization at a glance.

Health Summary

04

Time Management

⏰

Time Management Module

Manage to-dos, routines, and calendar in one place

  • Add a To-Do: Tap the + button → enter a title, due date, priority (High/Medium/Low), and category, then save
  • Mark as Complete: Tap the checkbox on the left of a to-do to mark it as done
  • Set Up Routines: Register activities you want to repeat daily (e.g., exercise, reading) as routines and check in every day
  • Streak Tracking: Consecutive days of completing a routine are counted as a streak
  • Calendar View: Switch between monthly, weekly, and daily views to see the flow of your schedule

To-Do Priority Guide

Priority Display When to Use
High Red highlight Must be completed today
Medium Yellow highlight Should be handled within the week
Low Default color Can be handled whenever time permits
05

Money Management

💰

Money Management Module

Track income and expenses, set budgets, view asset overview

  • Add an Expense: Tap + → enter amount, category (Food/Transport/Leisure, etc.), memo, and date
  • Record Income: Select "Income" as the type to save it as an income entry
  • Monthly Budget: Set a budget per category — a warning is shown when you go over
  • Spending Overview: View spending by category at a glance with a pie chart
  • Monthly Trend: See spending changes over the past 6 months in a line chart
  • Asset Overview: Manually enter cash, deposits, and investment assets to see a financial summary

Default Categories

🍽️ Food  ·  🚌 Transport  ·  🏠 Housing

🎮 Leisure  ·  👗 Shopping  ·  💊 Healthcare

📚 Education  ·  ✈️ Travel  ·  💸 Savings

+ You can also add your own custom categories

06

Health · Diet · Drinking Tracking

🥗

Health Tracking Module

Log diet, drinking, sleep, exercise, and body weight

  • Meal Log: Choose Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner / Snack → enter food name and calories. Daily total calories are automatically tallied
  • Drinking Log: Enter drink type, volume (ml), and number of servings. Weekly alcohol intake is automatically tracked
  • Sleep Log: Enter bedtime and wake-up time → total sleep duration is calculated automatically. You can also rate your sleep quality with stars
  • Exercise Log: Record exercise type, duration, and calories burned
  • Weight Log: Enter your weight and body fat percentage to view a trend chart of changes over time
⚠️

A warning banner is displayed when your weekly alcohol intake exceeds the WHO recommended limit (14 drinks for women, 21 drinks for men). This is a reminder feature to support healthy drinking habits.

07

Private Records

🔒

Private Records Module

Diary, emotions, relationships — protected by PIN

  • Write a Diary: Write diary entries by date and choose an emotion tag (10 options: 😊 😢 😡 😴, etc.)
  • Emotion Calendar: Emotion icons are displayed on the calendar by date, letting you track your emotional flow at a glance
  • Diary Search: Search past diary entries by keyword or emotion tag
  • Relationship Notes: Register people (family, friends, colleagues) and note the last time you met them and what you talked about

Setting Up PIN Lock

1

Tap the Private Records tab

The PIN setup screen appears the first time you access it.

2

Set a PIN

Set a 4–6 digit numeric PIN. Only you will know this number.

3

Enter PIN on each visit

You must enter your PIN every time you access Private Records. After 5 failed attempts, access is locked for 10 minutes.

🔐

Diary entries and relationship notes are stored encrypted. Even server administrators cannot read the content.

08

Account Management

Profile Settings

When you sign up with email, your avatar displays 'U'. Go to Settings → Profile and set a nickname to display your initials instead.

  1. Go to the Settings page
  2. Enter a nickname in the Profile section
  3. Click Save

Sign Out

Click the Settings → Account → Sign Out button to sign out immediately. You will be signed out automatically after 30 minutes of inactivity.

Delete Account

Click Settings → Account → Delete Account to request account deletion.

  • After requesting deletion, it will be processed after admin review
  • Data cannot be recovered after deletion
  • You can use the service with a nickname instead of your real name
09

Security & Privacy

🛡️

Complete Data Isolation

Your data is only visible to you. Other users of the same app — including family members — cannot access it.

🔐

Diary Encryption

Diary entries and relationship notes are stored encrypted with AES-256.

⏱️

Auto Lock

After 30 minutes of inactivity, the app locks automatically and requires you to log in again.

🌐

HTTPS Only

All communication is encrypted over HTTPS. Your data is safe even on public Wi-Fi.

10

Frequently Asked Questions

I forgot my password. ▼
Tap "Forgot your password?" on the login screen. A reset link will be sent to your registered email. If you signed up with Google, simply log in with your Google account.
I forgot my PIN. ▼
You can reset your PIN from the Settings menu. You will need to authenticate with your account password to reset it. Note that diary content is decrypted using the encryption key linked to your PIN — after resetting your PIN, you may not be able to decrypt previous diary entries, so please be careful.
Can my family members see my records? ▼
No. Each person's account is completely independent. Even if others use the same service, it is technically impossible to access another person's data.
Can I use it offline? ▼
If you add the app to your home screen (PWA), you can view the last synchronized data offline. However, saving new records requires an internet connection.
Can I back up or export my data? ▼
You can export all your data as a CSV or JSON file from the Settings menu. We recommend backing up your data regularly.
Can I use it on multiple devices at the same time? ▼
Yes! Log in with the same account from your smartphone, tablet, or PC and your synchronized data will be available anywhere.
The app is slow or showing errors. ▼
Try refreshing your browser, or fully close the app and reopen it. If the problem persists, try clearing your browser cache or switching to a different browser.
